The Dark Side of Digital Finance

A Poetic Tale of Vader's PayPal Adventures

In chambers dark where stars align,
Lord Vader ponders wealth divine.
His PayPal balance runs too low,
As battle plans begin to grow.

Through digital realms of ones and zeros,
Where credits flow like cosmic echoes,
He seeks a path to riches free,
While Death Star plans await with glee.

"The Force is strong with PayPal's might,
But empty balance brings such fright.
If only there were ways to gain,
Without the Emperor's watchful reign."

His breathing heavy, thoughts profound,
As notification alerts sound.
"Insufficient funds," they dare declare,
While troops await repairs with care.

But wisdom speaks through ancient code,
Of honest work and paths that showed,
That true wealth comes from effort pure,
Not shortcuts that might not endure.

So Vader learns in cosmic night,
That PayPal balance must be right.
Through proper means and labor true,
Not Force-tricks that he once knew.
